About Faunus Plant

We are a family business and we place above all the principles and values ​​passed down from generation to generation. The sense of community, love for people and respect for nature inspired our steps, bringing us closer to our greatest desire, to contribute to the well-being of those who cross our threshold. At the same time, we are actively involved in preserving the environment.

Thus, we chose to use the gifts of nature in remedies obtained by manual processing, using our own recipes. All our products are made with care for the medicinal plants used, but especially for the final consumer. The appreciation of the people who have tried them determines us to become better and better and to always enrich our portfolio.
